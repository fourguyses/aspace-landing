How it works

One coordinate.
A complete soil picture.

SoPrAna fuses multi-source satellite imagery — including Sentinel-2, Landsat, and commercial high-resolution providers — with an ensemble machine learning model trained on over one million georeferenced soil samples. The result is a field-resolution soil map generated in seconds, covering NPK, pH, organic matter, salinity, moisture, and more.

No field visits. No sampling kits. No lab turnaround. Just accurate, continuous data available via dashboard or API.
